Overview

Purpose

When you want to remodel your basement or your home what do you usually do? You grab a paper to draw and try to explain to your partner hoping he/she will visualize what you are planning to do. Does this work all the time? Probably not. That is where I come in. I create the blueprint of your house giving you a better idea of what your remodel will look like in the area you want to improve. I give different options with prices of the type of work you will like for your space with design of furniture and elevation in 3D. You will have the option on my website to select what part of your space you would like to see as a 3D model.

Audience

My intended audience are local homeowners or realtors who are looking to upgrade either a small or bigger area of their house or investment. Trying to visualize an idea and share with others is difficult and can cause stress for the homeowner and family, causing delays in the remodeling process. I will try to make this website friendly and simple so everyone from different ages and backgrounds can understand what we are offering and have access from different devices.

Popular areas of your home to remodel

Kitchen: Updating the kitchen is one of the most common areas of a home to remodel. By doing this, it adds value to your home as well as give your home an updated feel. If you are unsure of what you could change, at S&A Drafting and Designs, we can make professional recommendations to you that are both big and small so you can choose which options are best for you.

Living Room: Another room in the house that people spend the most time in is the living room. In addition to the kitchen, the living room is a place to gather with family and friends. We can help this place be more enjoyable for you and your family by adding square footage, creating an open floor plan, or anything else your heart could desire. We are here to help you build a new room so you can build memories.

Family Room:Adding a great room to any home gives an open concept to the home that is highly sought after. Many great rooms include high ceilings such as vaulted ceilings and will add appeal and character to you home. This space is very versatile and will give your home an up to date look and feel.
