Popular areas of your home to remodel

Kitchen

Updating the kitchen is one of the most common areas of a home to remodel. By doing this, it adds value to your home as well as give your home an updated feel. If you are unsure of what you could change, at S&A Drafting and Designs, we can make professional recommendations to you that are both big and small so you can choose which options are best for you.

Living Room

Another room in the house that people spend the most time in is the living room. In addition to the kitchen, the living room is a place to gather with family and friends. We can help this place be more enjoyable for you and your family by adding square footage, creating an open floor plan, or anything else your heart could desire. We are here to help you build a new room so you can build memories.

Family Room

Adding a great room to any home gives an open concept to the home that is highly sought after. Many great rooms include high ceilings such as vaulted ceilings and will add appeal and character to you home. This space is very versatile and will give your home an up to date look and feel.
